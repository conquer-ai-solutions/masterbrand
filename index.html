The Solution

Finance Shared Services Copilot

Two high-impact starting points: an AR dashboard that answers questions like "why did the top 10 past due change?" without a single pivot table, and a vendor statement reconciliation engine that replaces the manual Click-to-Excel workflow with AI-powered matching across Oracle and Friedman.

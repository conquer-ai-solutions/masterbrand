What We Heard

Two problems.
One platform.

In our discovery session, MasterBrand's Finance Shared Services team described two interconnected challenges that are consuming team capacity and blocking AI progress. Both are solvable. This platform addresses them together.

📊

AR is a Reporting Dead End

The AR dashboard pulls from Snowflake, but it's static. Every time a VP asks "why did our top 10 past-due customers change vs. last quarter?" — someone spends 45 minutes manually compiling data from multiple ClickSense dashboards, downloading to Excel, and building a one-off answer. There's no trend intelligence, no proactive alerting, and no way to just ask the data a question.

~45 min
to answer a single AR question today
🔄

AP Double Entry — Every Invoice, Every Day

The Invoice Hub (Power Apps) was supposed to automate invoice processing. OCR training failed when vendors changed their templates. Today the AP team manually enters invoice data twice: once into Power Apps, once into Oracle. Exceptions live in email threads across three separate data sources — ClickSense holds, ClickSense processed/unprocessed, and the hub itself.

3 systems
manually reconciled for every exception
The scenario we'll follow today

Tuesday morning, MasterBrand Shared Services. Hendrick's Building Supply — a top-10 customer — has moved $847,000 into the 90-day past-due bucket. Patrick needs to know why it changed vs. Q1 last year, but the ClickSense dashboard can't tell him. Meanwhile, 23 invoices from Amerock Hardware are sitting in the AP exception queue blocking $312,000 in payments. The GRN in Oracle doesn't match the invoice quantity. The team is managing it on email. We'll follow this through every screen.
